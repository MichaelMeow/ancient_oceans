Book Recent Work Prints

My name is Matt Quinn and I photograph landscapes under the stars with the aim of inpsiring a sense of wonder about the natural world around us. As our lives become more entangled with technology everyday, I believe it’s never been more important to reignite our relationship with nature. Through the dark sky experience, I believe we find a greater sense of belonging and connectedness with the world around us. The sheer scale of the night sky puts into perspective the awesomeness we're a part of and with this new perspective I'm reminded of what's truely important and what isn't.
